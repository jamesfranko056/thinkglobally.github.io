As a developer, supporting different languages comes with a bit of overhead that you have to design for

Another important goal of Noto is to provide harmonius look and feel across languages. To achive harmony is not as simple as setting the same height for all languages. For example, tall languages, like Tibetian in this image, will look really squeezed and much harder to read than English, although the same height for English and Tibetian is actually set.
